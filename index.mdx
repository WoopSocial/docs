WoopSocial is a social media management platform. You can use it to:
* Create and schedule posts on various social platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, LinkedIn Pages, Pinterest, TikTok, X (formerly Twitter) and YouTube through our UI
* Automate your workflows through our powerful API
* Give your AI agent the ability to manage your socials via our MCP
